E-Quiz Web Based Application (reactJs ,springboot, restapi, jpa, hibernate, mySQL)

We have designed this web based application with the purpose of allowing the students to give quiz and view their results.  
This application is an attempt to remove the existing flaws in the manual system of conducting exams. 
Faculties are be able to create Quiz's of various topics and can schedule them. The performance of the student will  be  fully  monitored  by Faculty.  
Faculty can manage the quiz in which he can add, delete or update the questions in the quiz.
Students can view the quiz schedule in his/her profile after login into their particular account in system.
Students are provided the flexibility to choose the quiz among different topics and can view their result.
